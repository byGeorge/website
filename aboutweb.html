Hosting
Amazon Web Services (AWS). This is an absolutely fabulous resource that scales easily. Tiny projects like this have very minimal cost, and you can set limits on scaling so your website doesn't accidentally get a Reddit hug that costs tens of thousands of dollars. If you're part of a larger corporation, you can quickly scale applications to accommodate surges in consumer activity (if a billion people show up to shop at your site on Black Friday, your website will not go down).
